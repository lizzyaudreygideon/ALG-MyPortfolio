About me

Audrey Gideon - Front-end Web Developer

I craft engaging web experiences with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React. Volunteering as a Scratch instructor fostered creativity in a number of students. Committed learner eager to collaborate and build impactful websites.

What if your website could be more than just a page?


I'm Audrey Gideon, an aspiring web developer with a passion for crafting engaging and interactive online experiences. My foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript fuels my drive to build websites that not only look great but also function flawlessly. Beyond technical skills, I believe in the power of collaboration.


My experience as a volunteer Scratch instructor at Uncommon.org has allowed me to share my knowledge with students, fostering their creativity and problem-solving abilities. This experience, coupled with completing a project management course in Agile and Waterfall methodologies, has equipped me with the skills to seamlessly navigate any project, big or small.


I'm a continuous learner, always eager to expand my skillset and tackle new challenges. I'm confident that my technical expertise and project management abilities, combined with my collaborative spirit, will be a valuable asset to any team.
